How to make cannoli!

Cannoli is a Sicilian dessert

Below you will find the ingredients in order to make this delicious dessert

Ingredients:

For cannoli Shells

  • Unbleached all-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Marsala wine
  • Egg and egg white
  • Vegetable oil

For cannoli filling

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Powder sugar
  • Pistacchio nuts
  • Mini chocolate chips

Steps:

  • Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  • Add in butter
  • Add Marsala wine
  • Add the eggs
  • When the dough is formed, let it rest for about 30 minutes
  • Heat the oil in a pot
  • Roll the dough and form and cut it into smaller ovals
  • Wrap the ovals and form the cannolo
  • Fry them in the oil until golden brown
  • Remove the shells from the form and dry from oil
  • Make the filling
  • Once shells are cool, fill them in with the cannoli filling
