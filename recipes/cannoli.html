How to make cannoli!

Cannoli is a Sicilian dessert

Below you will find the ingredients in order to make this delicious dessert

Ingredients:

For cannoli Shells

  • Unbleached all-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Marsala wine
  • Egg and egg white
  • Vegetable oil

For cannoli filling

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Powder sugar
  • Pistacchio nuts
  • Mini chocolate chips
